Career Aspirations

  • AutoCAD Proficiency
  • Site Surveying
  • Minute taking

  • Coordinating Services
  • Communicating with external project members
  • Managing project requirements
  • Report Writing

What I would like to be doing

I am studying programming in my free time to facilitate a career change into Software Development. at this point, my goal is to progress into a role as a full stack developer in a software consultancy company as I have prior experience within consultancy work. At first, I seemed to prefer backend development, as my main interests were how things worked 'under the hood'. However, as I have learnt more about the industry and seen how things work, I have definitely gained an appreciation for front end development, and find both parts equally satisying at this point. Whilst learning about how programs run and interact with each other, I have gained an interest in the principles of how computers work. For example, working on projects which involved declaring ports for a PostgreSQL database and Python program to interact, introduced me to how deeply you can dive into learning in this industry - a strong reason which led me to this career change. Another example of this is the setup of a secure connection to access email accounts, and the communication protocols required.

Of course I understand that I do not fully understand the industry and I am sure that as I learn more and gain employment within relevant roles that my goals will change, however slightly. Overall I am very excited for the future and to learn more!
